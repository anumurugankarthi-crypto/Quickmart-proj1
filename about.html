Our Story

QuickMart was founded in 2021 with one simple idea — groceries should arrive before you even finish thinking about what to cook. We started in Chennai with a single dark store and 5 delivery partners.

Today, we operate across 50+ cities with 500+ dark stores and 10,000+ delivery heroes.

We work directly with farmers, local vendors, and trusted brands to bring you the freshest produce at the lowest prices. No middlemen. No delays. Just fresh groceries at your door in 10 minutes.

Our Values

Speed First

We built our entire infrastructure around one goal — 10-minute delivery. Every warehouse, every route, every hire is optimized for speed.

Always Fresh

Our inventory is sourced daily from local farms and suppliers. If it isn't fresh, it doesn't reach your door. Simple as that.

Lowest Prices

We cut out the middleman and pass the savings to you. Best prices guaranteed on all groceries, every single day.

Community First

We support local farmers and small businesses. Every order you place helps the communities we serve grow stronger.
