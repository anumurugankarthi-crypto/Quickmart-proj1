Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

We respect your privacy. Your data is never sold to third parties and is only used to improve your QuickMart experience.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number and date of birth
  • Delivery address and location (only with your permission)
  • Order history, preferences and spending patterns
  • Payment details — stored securely, never in plain text
  • Device info — IP address, browser type and app version

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders within 10 minutes
  • To send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • To personalise product recommendations for you
  • To process payments and prevent fraud
  • To send offers and discounts — you can opt out anytime

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data. We only share limited information with:

  • Delivery partners — name, address and phone to complete delivery
  • Payment processors — to handle your transactions securely
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law or court order

Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in, remember your cart, and understand how you use our platform. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correct — Update your information from profile settings
  • Delete — Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out — Unsubscribe from marketing emails or SMS anytime

Data Security

All data is protected using SSL/TLS encryption. Payments are processed on PCI-compliant secure servers. We run regular security audits and only authorised staff can access your data.

Questions about your privacy?

Our team is happy to help. Reach us at privacy@quickmart.in or call 1800-123-4567. We respond within 2 business days.
